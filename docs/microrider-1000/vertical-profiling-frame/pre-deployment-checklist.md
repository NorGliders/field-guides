# Pre-deployment Checklist

Complete the following checks immediately before deploying the MicroRider-1000.

!!! warning "Fragile microstructure probes"

    The shear and FP07 temperature probes are extremely fragile. Even light contact with fingers, clothing or tools can permanently damage the probe tips. Always handle the frame carefully and keep personnel clear of the probe end during deployment and recovery.

---

## 1. Final Inspection

Confirm that:

- [ ] MicroRider is securely mounted.
- [ ] External battery is securely mounted.
- [ ] Battery Y-cable is connected.
- [ ] Frame guards are installed.
- [ ] All probe holder caps are tightened.
- [ ] Probe serial numbers match the instrument configuration.
- [ ] Shear probes are correctly aligned.
- [ ] Probe tips are free from visible damage.

---

## 2. Install the Shorting Plug

<photo>

1. Remove the communication cable from the MicroRider.
2. Inspect the connector and O-rings for contamination or damage.
3. Apply a light coating of **DC4 silicone grease** to the connector faces and O-rings.
4. Retrieve the shorting plug from the **spares kit** stored in the transport case.
5. Install the shorting plug and hand-tighten the locking sleeve.

!!! important "Power on"

    Installing the shorting plug powers on the MicroRider and starts data acquisition. The instrument is now ready for deployment.

---

## 3. Deploy

Once the shorting plug has been installed:

- [ ] Ensure all personnel are clear of the probe end.
- [ ] Lower the frame carefully into the water.
- [ ] Avoid all contact with the microstructure probes during handling.

---

## Recovery

!!! warning "Probe protection"

    Exercise the same level of care during recovery. Avoid contact with the probe tips while lifting, handling or securing the frame on deck.

After recovery:

1. Lay the frame horizontally on a pallet or suitable support.
2. Remove the shorting plug.
3. Refit the communication cable.

!!! important "Power off"

    Removing the shorting plug powers off the MicroRider and closes the data file for the completed profile.

## Final Check

- [ ] Shorting plug installed before deployment.
- [ ] Instrument deployed without probe contact.
- [ ] Shorting plug removed after recovery.
- [ ] Communication cable reconnected.
- [ ] Frame stored horizontally awaiting data download or probe removal.

# Cleaning and Storage

Clean the MicroRider-1000 immediately after recovery and return the instrument to its transport case.

!!! warning "Fragile probes"

    The shear probes and FP07 temperature probes are extremely fragile. Handle the instrument carefully and avoid all contact with the probe tips during cleaning and storage.

---

## 1. Rinse the Instrument

<photo>

1. Rinse the complete instrument and frame thoroughly with fresh water.
2. Remove all traces of salt and debris from the frame, connectors and mounting hardware.
3. Allow the instrument to drain before removing the probes.

!!! important

    Do not use high-pressure water directly on the microstructure probes or bulkhead connectors.

---

## 2. Remove the Microstructure Probes

<photo>

Remove the probes in the reverse order of installation.

1. Loosen the probe holder cap using the probe installation tool.
2. Carefully withdraw the probe until the SMC connector is accessible.
3. Hold the SMC connector body and disconnect the probe.
4. Immediately fit the protective sleeve to the probe.
5. Store the probe in the probe case.
6. Repeat for all remaining probes.

!!! important

    Remove the **FP07 temperature probes first**, followed by the **shear probes**, to minimise the risk of accidental damage.

---

## 3. Install the Test Probes

<photo>

1. Inspect the ferrule and O-ring on each test probe.
2. Connect the SMC connector.
3. Carefully insert the test probe into the probe holder.
4. Finger-tighten the probe holder cap.
5. Tighten the cap using the probe installation tool.

---

## 4. Prepare for Storage

<photo>

1. Dry all bulkhead connectors using clean compressed air or Kimwipes.
2. Apply a light coating of **DC4 silicone grease** to all exposed connector faces and O-rings.
3. Fit all dummy connectors.
4. Inspect the instrument for damage.

---

## 5. Store the Instrument

<photo>

1. Disconnect the external battery.
2. Remove the MicroRider and external battery from the frame if required.
3. Return all equipment to the transport case.
4. Store the transport case in a clean, dry location.

## Final Check

- [ ] Instrument rinsed with fresh water.
- [ ] Deployment probes removed and stored.
- [ ] Test probes installed.
- [ ] Dummy connectors fitted.
- [ ] Connectors cleaned and greased.
- [ ] Instrument inspected for damage.
- [ ] Equipment returned to the transport case.
